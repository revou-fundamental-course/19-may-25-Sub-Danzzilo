Temperature Converter







Explanation

To convert between different temperature scales, you can use the following formulas:

Celsius (°C)
  • Celsius to Fahrenheit: F = C × ⅔ + 32
  • Celsius to Kelvin: K = C + 273.15
  • Celsius to Reaumur: Ré = C × ⅕
Fahrenheit (°F)
  • Fahrenheit to Celsius: C = (F − 32) × ⅕
  • Fahrenheit to Kelvin: K = (F − 32) × ⅕ + 273.15
  • Fahrenheit to Reaumur: Ré = (F − 32) × ⅙
Kelvin (°K)
  • Kelvin to Celsius: C = K − 273.15
  • Kelvin to Fahrenheit: F = (K − 273.15) × ⅔ + 32
  • Kelvin to Reaumur: Ré = (K − 273.15) × ⅕
Reaumur (°Ré)
  • Reaumur to Celsius: C = Ré × ⅕
  • Reaumur to Fahrenheit: F = Ré × ⅙ + 32
  • Reaumur to Kelvin: K = Ré × ⅕ + 273.15

Note: The formulas above are based on the definitions of the temperature scales.

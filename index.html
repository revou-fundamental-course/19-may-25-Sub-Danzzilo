Temperature Converter





Explanation

To convert between different temperature scales, you can use the following formulas:

Celsius (°C)
  • Celsius to Fahrenheit: °F = °C × 9⁄5 + 32
  • Celsius to Kelvin: °K = °C + 273.15
  • Celsius to Reaumur: °Ré = °C × 4⁄5
Fahrenheit (°F)
  • Fahrenheit to Celsius: °C = (°F − 32) × 5⁄9
  • Fahrenheit to Kelvin: °K = (°F − 32) × 5⁄9 + 273.15
  • Fahrenheit to Reaumur: °Ré = (°F − 32) × 4⁄9
Kelvin (°K)
  • Kelvin to Celsius: °C = °K − 273.15
  • Kelvin to Fahrenheit: °F = (°K − 273.15) × 9⁄5 + 32
  • Kelvin to Reaumur: °Ré = (°K − 273.15) × 4⁄5
Reaumur (°Ré)
  • Reaumur to Celsius: °C = °Ré × 5⁄4
  • Reaumur to Fahrenheit: °F = °Ré × 9⁄4 + 32
  • Reaumur to Kelvin: °K = °Ré × 5⁄4 + 273.15

Note: The formulas above are based on the definitions of the temperature scales.
